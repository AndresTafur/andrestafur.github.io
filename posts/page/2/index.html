Microservice constellation

A microservices constellation refers to the architectural style of using multiple independent, autonomous microservices to form a larger, coordinated system, often seen in complex software platforms like flight systems or confidential Kubernetes environments. The term “constellation” highlights the unified, yet distributed, nature of these services, which communicate and collaborate to achieve a common goal, similar to how individual spacecraft in a space constellation work together.
