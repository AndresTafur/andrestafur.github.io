When you build a C program, a lot happens before and after your main():

Entry point The kernel loads your program at _start (not main). _start is provided by crt1.o (part of glibc or musl).

Startup glue _start sets up the stack, argc/argv, environment, TLS, etc. Then it calls into __libc_start_main(main, …) in glibc.
